Iota Gamma

Iota Gamma on Caldwell university chartered December 3rd started because of 5 members Nathan Adu Nkansa Dikwa - Nkrumah,Mariano McGaskey ,Randy Antenor,Ernesto Hernandez,Kevon Bryant. Iota Gamma chapter service to the community reflects its commitment to making a positive impact beyond its members. Through initiatives such as mentoring youth, organizing food drives, hosting educational workshops, and supporting local nonprofits, the chapter fosters meaningful change. These efforts not only address critical community needs but also embody the fraternity’s values of leadership, service, and brotherhood, inspiring others to join in creating a stronger, more connected society.Brotherhood within the iota gamma chapter is the foundation of lifelong connections built on trust, shared experiences, and mutual support. It fosters an environment where members uplift one another through challenges, celebrate each other’s successes, and work together toward common goals. Beyond camaraderie, brotherhood cultivates personal growth, accountability, and a sense of belonging, creating bonds that extend far beyond college and into every stage of life.
